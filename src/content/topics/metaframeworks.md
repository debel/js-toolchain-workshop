## Why use a meta frameworks

Meta frameworks aim to provide a cohesive, well-structured, easy-to-setup experience for bootstrapping projects:

- They eliminate the need to curate each tool that you would need.
- Provide a solution to most common problems out-of-the-box.
- Provide support through dedicated communities and plugins.

## What do meta frameworks give you?

- Include & setup a rendering frameworks
- Server-side rendering
- Static site generation
- Utilities for common problems (forms, streaming, async loading, etc)

## Some popular meta frameworks include

- Deno fresh
- Tanstack-start
- Astro
- Svelte-kit
- Nuxt?
- Next.js (not vite based)
